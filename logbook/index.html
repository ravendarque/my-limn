⚠️ Grading Notes

Southern Sandstone: Tech grade system — feels ~2 grades harder than Font equivalent. A Font 6B here will feel like a Font 7A elsewhere. Do not be surprised.

Northumberland sandstone: Notoriously sandbagged. Treat every grade as a half-grade harder than labelled.
